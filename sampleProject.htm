Tableau Dashboard: Healthcare Data

2021

This interactive dashboard showcases the min/max values for copay and Medicare prices for new vs. established patients filtered by zipcode. The input data consists of an Emergency Medicine dataset from The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services.
